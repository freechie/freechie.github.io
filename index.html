About

I'm a software engineer based in New Jersey and New York with a B.S. in Computer Science and experience in data management, database systems, and full-stack web development.

Until 2022, I worked with Columbia University's Data Management Group, maintaining Athena, the system of record for 5M+ alumni, donors, and organizations. I exported, cleaned, and re-imported data using SQL, Python, and Excel, built PL/SQL views for reporting, and mapped family relationships to support multi-generational outreach. I also audited records, integrated external data sources, and helped reduce the "lost" constituent population—those with no active contact information or who may be deceased—from 18% in 2017 to under 4% by 2022.

I contributed to data governance, reporting, and BI dashboards that supported The Columbia Commitment campaign, which raised $5.48B. I'm currently building projects in C/C++, Python, and JavaScript while continuing to strengthen my data structures and algorithms skills through MIT OpenCourseWare.
