About

I'm a software engineer based in New Jersey and New York with a background in Data Management and Computer Science. Until 2022, I worked with Columbia University's Data Management Group, managing and querying databases with over 5 million constituent records in support of major fundraising campaigns.

Using SQL, Python, and Excel, I exported, cleaned, and re-imported data to improve accuracy and usability. I also tested and refined tables, views, and headers to better support development needs. Alongside this, I integrated data from sources like Lexis, USPS, and LinkedIn, and built relationship graphs to map giving history across families and generations for outreach prioritization.

I improved data quality by auditing systems and contributing to data governance efforts, helping reduce the university's "lost" constituent population from over 11% to under 4%.

I hold a B.S. in Computer Science from Montclair State University and am currently building projects while looking for an opportunity to grow and contribute as an engineer.
